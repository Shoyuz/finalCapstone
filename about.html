About me

My roots as a passionate wildlife enthusiast, originate from my country of birth, Tanzania. Having being born and raised in Tanzania before moving to the United KingdoM, i was lucky enough to grow up surrounded by the untammed African wilderness. My experiences as a child growing up in rural Tanzania have always stayed with me to this day and have helped shape me into who i am today. I love everything connected with the African wilderness; the animals, the birds, the insects , the plants and the people. Being out there in the wild and connecting with nature, is an experience that everyone of us should have in our life time. It reminds us of who we are, where we have come from, and why it is critical to preserve and protect our precious wilderness areas. We owe it to the next generation to have the chance to experience and see these wonders of nature that our generation is blessed with, and more than ever before these precious wildlife resources are under immesnse pressure of survival from our ever growing population and the competition for resources. My mission is to showcase to you some of the of most magnificient wilderness areas in my country of birth, Tanzania.
